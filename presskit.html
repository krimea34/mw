About Mimmox

Mimmox, founded in 2024, is an indie game studio crafting cozy, heartwarming experiences. Our games aim to soothe and delight, helping players unwind. UMAMI is our debut title, kicking off our mission to create playful, soulful games that comfort the spirit.

About UMAMI

UMAMI is a cozy 3D food puzzle game with handcrafted levels. Tinker, rotate, and fit blocks to build food towers and cakes in a soothing atmosphere.
